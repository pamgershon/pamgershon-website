Welcome

Dr. Pam Gershon is a licensed psychologist and certified school psychologist in New York State. She specializes in therapy for children, adolescents, and adults, as well as parent guidance and counseling focusing on the following issues:

  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Fears/Phobias
  • ADHD
  • Behavioral issues
  • Social Issues
  • School Issues
  • Obsessive Compulsive Disorder
  • Autism Spectrum Disorder
  • Stress
  • Divorce

About Dr. Gershon

Dr. Gershon holds an M.S. in School Psychology and a Psy.D. in Clinical/School Psychology from New York University. She has been in practice for 29 years and has interned, trained, and worked in a variety of school, hospital, and clinical settings.

In addition, Dr. Gershon is currently a Doctoral Lecturer in the Queens College School Psychology Program, where she supervises graduate students in Cognitive Behavioral and Play Therapy.

Services

Dr. Gershon offers both teletherapy services and home-based services. Her therapeutic approach is eclectic and client-centered utilizing principles and techniques of:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy
  • Behavior Therapy
  • Mindfulness
  • Social Skills Training
  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy
  • Play Therapy

Fees and Insurance

Dr. Gershon does not participate with any insurance plans, so clients pay her directly at the time of service. However, because she is a Licensed Psychologist, many clients receive some level of reimbursement for her services.

You may want to ask your insurance company about your coverage for individual therapy with an "out of network" Licensed Psychologist.

The fee is based on the length of your session. Most clients come once a week for a 50-minute session, but some clients find it useful to schedule longer or more frequent sessions or sessions every two weeks. Please contact Dr. Gershon for current fee information.
